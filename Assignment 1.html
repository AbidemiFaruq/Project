Starting a Narrative Essay

From the narrations disscussed so far in the two study units,it should be clear that a narrative essay can
begin anywhere depending on what part of the story of the writer wishes to emphasise.

There are three options.

  1. You can start from the beginning of the story,as in the story of "Bimbo and the fish" on page 21
  2. You can start from the climax of the story, as in the story of "Bisi admitted in hospital as a result
    of an accident" on page 41
  3. You can start from the end of the story, as in the school child's narration to his father about his
    school day.

Whichever you choose,make your intoduction interesting.

Important Points to Note

  • To write a good narrative essay, you must ensure you understand the topic.
  • You must ensure your immagination is sharp enough to build up a plausible,interestind story,factual
    or fictitious,very fast and in sequence
  • You must ensure you have a good controlof tenses and can use past tenses adequately
